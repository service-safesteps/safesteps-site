What we help with

Everything your setting needs to stay safe and compliant.

Health and safety support built around the realities of running a nursery or school. Practical guidance to help you stay safe, compliant, and confident.

Risk assessments

Clear, site-specific risk assessments covering premises, activities, workstations and more, written in plain English.

Policies & documentation

Up-to-date health & safety policies, procedures and records that meet your legal obligations and are ready the moment an inspector asks.

Compliance audits & checks

Regular reviews that keep you on top of changing rules, with short, jargon-free reports you can act on straight away.

Staff guidance & support

Straightforward advice and training for your team, so safety becomes part of everyday practice instead of a last-minute scramble.

Ongoing support

Optional ongoing support, so you have a qualified specialist on hand whenever a question or a change comes up.

Inspection-readiness reviews

A walkthrough of your setting against current standards, with a clear, prioritised action list, so you know exactly what needs doing before an inspection.

A few specialist jobs sit outside what we do, such as full fire risk assessments, asbestos surveys and legionella assessments. Where you need those, we are happy to point you towards the right people.
